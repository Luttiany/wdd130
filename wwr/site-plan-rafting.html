Purpose

The purpose of a rafting website is to provide information, resources, and services related to rafting adventures, such as guided trips, gear rentals, courses, and customized experiences, to attract and facilitate bookings from adventure seekers.

Audience

Overall, the audience comprises anyone with an interest in rafting, ranging from novices to experienced enthusiasts, who are seeking adventure, fun, and memorable experiences on the water.
